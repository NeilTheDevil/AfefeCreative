Common Questions

Do you charge an emergency call-out fee?
No. We value transparency. Our dispatch fee is $49, which gets waived if you proceed with our quoted repairs. Night or day, the diagnostic fee remains the same.
How fast can you get here?
For true emergencies (active leaks, entirely clogged main lines, no hot water), we guarantee a 1-hour response time within our primary service area.
Are your plumbers licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Every technician is fully licensed, background-checked, and highly insured. Your property is entirely protected while we work.

Yes, for larger installations like new tankless water heaters or full repipes, we offer flexible financing options starting at 0% APR for qualified homeowners.
