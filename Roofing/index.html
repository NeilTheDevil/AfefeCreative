Clear Answers

Everything you need to know about your roofing project.

How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1 to 2 days, depending on the size and complexity of the structure. We prioritize speed without compromising on safety or quality.
Will my insurance cover the full cost?
If your roof has suffered verifiable storm or wind damage, your provider typically covers the entire cost minus your deductible. Our specialists work to maximize your coverage.
What materials do you recommend?
We offer architectural shingles, metal roofing, and composite slate. The best choice depends on your budget, house style, and desired energy efficiency goals.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, Apex Roofing is fully licensed in the state and carries multi-million dollar general liability and workers' compensation insurance for your protection.
